You’re in good hands

On October 19, 1987, a day known as “Black Monday”, an agency called CJ Advertising opened its doors on Mill Street with three clients and high hopes.

That it weathered that inauspicious beginning and subsequently flourished is testimony to the optimism, enthusiasm, and tenacity of its founder Connie Jo Miller. Twenty-six years later, CJ Advertising has evolved into Group CJ, with an expanded menu of services encompassing branding, traditional marketing, P.R., broadcast production and full service digital marketing.

Artful communication. Results driven.

Our work consistently wins awards, but more importantly earns raves from the only judges that really count – our clients. What keeps them coming back for more? Regardless of size, the answer tends to be unanimous: big ideas, stellar creative and demonstrable results.
